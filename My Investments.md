# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the My Investments mobile application (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## No Data Collection

**We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your personal information or financial data on external servers.** 

All the data you input into the App—including your financial portfolio, investment details, and any other information—remains exclusively on your local device. 

## Biometric Authentication

If you choose to enable biometric authentication (such as Face ID or Touch ID) to secure the App, this process is handled entirely by your device's native operating system. We do not have access to, nor do we collect or store, any of your biometric data.

## Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or crash reporting tools that collect data about your usage or device. We do not track your activity within the App.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at thirdimpactapps@gmail.com.
